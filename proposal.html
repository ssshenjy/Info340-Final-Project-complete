Problem/Data Domain

  • Many people face challenges when it comes to travel planning. Travelers often struggle to consolidate their travel information from various sources and platforms, which can lead to information overload and decision paralysis. The travel planner app that serves as a reminder and allows users to maintain organization and effectively manage their travel plans, bookings, itineraries, accommodations, transportation, activities, and other important details. It offers features that enable users to track their expenses and receive timely reminders throughout their journeys.
  • An information technology problem in the travel planner space could be the integration and synchronization of data across multiple platforms and devices. For example, users might face challenges when trying to sync their travel plans and bookings seamlessly between the web app, mobile app, and other devices. Inconsistencies or delays in data updates can lead to confusion and disruptions in travel plans.

    App Description

    Potential users for the application are travelers pursuing a seamless and enjoyable vacation or business travels with a well-planned traveler plan. Individual travelers who are planning to travel without a travel agency can plan their destination of interest in the view of a calendar for the most personalized experience. Those who are seeking for efficient organization and the ability to work on their plans over a longer time span without going back and forth to ensure no dates are left out without a place to stay are highly encouraged to take advantage of the features of this web app. Users seek a solution to reduce tension and pressure of planning a trip by keeping their scattered notes in one place and releasing pressure on attention to detail. The information the users will be viewing will be based on the input provided by the users themselves. The app can thereby present information in an easily accessible manner.

    The main purpose of the web app is to provide users with a powerful platform to create and manage their travel schedules efficiently. It presents the schedules in an attractive and organized way, making them easy to understand and access.

    • Create a formatted schedule: Users can create a new schedule by inputting the travel dates, destinations, flight times, and etc. The web app will create a formatted schedule based on the input data.
    • Set time allocations: Users can specify the start and end time for each event and their expected time slots on each destination and activities in the schedule to make sure the plan is in the expected path.
    • Add on details and notes: Users can add specific notes on each activity in the schedule. It allows users to add address, contact information, and any other important information to enable an enjoyable travel experience.

    Planning a trip can often be complicated, involving extensive research, revising planned destinations, and booking hotels and activities on multiple platforms for each day of the trip. However, with the help of this web application, users can simplify the process. They can accumulate ideas and destinations over a longer time span, ensuring nothing is forgotten. By viewing the empty spots in their itinerary, users were reminded to fill-in those gaps and prompted to book hotels for specific days, thereby reducing the chances of missing important details or encountering last-minute challenges and ensuring a hassle-free trip planning experience. The key vision is to provide users with peace of mind throughout the process of both planning and traveling. It allows users to focus on the exciting aspects of their trip, leveraging their holistic experience over the traveling system.
